Web Development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves a range of tasks and technologies, including the design and layout of the website, the development of the underlying functionality, and the management and maintenance of the site. Some common technologies used in web development include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various programming languages such as Python, Ruby, and PHP. Web developers may work on the front-end, which refers to the visible portion of a website that users interact with, or the back-end, which involves the server-side logic and databases that power the website. Web development is a rapidly evolving field, and new tools and technologies are constantly being introduced. To be successful in web development, it's important to stay up to date with the latest trends and best practices.
